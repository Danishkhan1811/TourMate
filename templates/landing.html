About Us

TourMate aims to eliminate the need of having a tour guide by your side at all times and save the hefty amount of money spent on guides and travel agents due to the unavailability of such applications. This also eliminates the risks that come with travelling alongside a guide and gives you the freedom of travelling at your will without depending on someone else. Now you can visit places depending on your own comfort. With no major ‘all in one’ applications available for a tourist, TourMate is here to fill that void and provide a cost effective & easily accessible tool.
